By Cameron Roberts

Feb-Mar 2019



Systolic/Diastolic Glucose Pulse Ox Well Being
Systolic is where the heart contracts and pushes blood out, Diastolic is where the heart expands and pulls blood in. Glucose is your blood's sugar level. Pulse ox is how efficiently your blood carries oxygen from your lungs to the rest of your body. Well being is how well you feel. 100% is no pain, you can walk, talk, etc.
