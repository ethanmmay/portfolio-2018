Countdown to Summer by Ethan May

Summer Vacation is almost here! We can almost taste it as we work hard to get the grades we deserve. Over a stressful year of learning and developing skills, students are excited to catch a break, let loose and have some fun! Summer vacation is the perfect time to go on trips in planes, trains, boats and more! But that doesn't mean the learning stops! Summer camps built for learning are also exciting! Outdoor camps or even religious camps can be loads of fun during the summer. Even though the sun feels good on your skin, don't forget to wear sunblock so the fun doesn't spoil later with sunburnt skin!
