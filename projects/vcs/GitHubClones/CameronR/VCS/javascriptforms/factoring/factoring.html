Solve using factoring:

x2+8x+12

  1. If equation is not a quadratic, use factoring and synthetic division to make it one

  2. find all the factors of c(third coefficient), which are 1 and 12, 3 and 4, and 6 and 2

  3. Find the factors which add to 8, which are 6 and 2.

  4. 6*2 = 12, and 6+2 = 8, so (x+6) and (x+2) are factors.
